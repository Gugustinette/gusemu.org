# Frontends

Recently, there has been a rise in the popularity of [frontends](/guides/ressources/glossary#frontend), specially for Android devices.

These are applications that provide a unified interface for managing and launching your games across multiple emulators.

Here are the most popular ones:

- [iiSU](https://iisu.network/)
- [Cocoon](https://github.com/inssekt/CocoonFE)

<!-- @ignore-check-links -->
- [ES-DE](https://es-de.org/)

<!-- prettier-ignore-end -->

- [Daijishō](https://github.com/TapiocaFox/Daijishou)

Most of the time, they replace your device's default launcher, and provide a more console-like experience, with features like game metadata, box art, and more.

Metadata and box art are usually fetched from such onlines databases (which requires some setup and configuration) :

- [IGDB](https://www.igdb.com/)
- [SteamGridDB](https://www.steamgriddb.com/)
- [TheGamesDB](https://thegamesdb.net/)
- [Screenscraper](https://www.screenscraper.fr/)
